About Me: I am a Ph.D student at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata under the supervision of Prof. Utpal Garain. Presently, my Ph.D thesis has been submitted and I am waiting for the defense.


Research Interests: Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning.


Ph.D Topic: Lemmatization and some other morphological processing tasks for Indic languages. It focuses on the development of lemmatization methods and morphological analyzers for Indic languages. Their applications on several high-end NLP tasks such as semantics, word sense disambiguation are also studied. Initially unsupervised methods have been explored to solve the problems and they are applied on Bengali and Hindi. Later on, following the global state-of-the-art, deep learning based supervised techniques are designed and tried on world wide languages such as Catalan, Dutch, Hungarian, Italian, Latin, Romanian and Spanish.
